THE OPEN EFFECTS ASSOCIATION WELCOMES PLUG-IN DEVELOPERS TO ATTEND ANNUAL MEETING

Developers discuss future of OpenFX for video processing and VFX plug-ins

WEB BASED MEETING — The Open Effects Association (OFXA), a non-profit organization that promotes open standards across the visual effects community, announces it will be holding its annual general meeting on Tuesday, April 21, 2020. This year due to current events the meeting will be happening online. Attendance is open to all, and attendees will have an opportunity to network with plug-in developers from the top visual effects providers.

For Zoom meeting login details, please ask a member or email info@openeffects.org.

Agenda Summary: 2 hours

  1. AGM – Association Business (short)
  2. State of the API (rest of first hour)
  3. Special Session on GPU (1 hour)

About OFXA

The Open Effects Association (OFXA) is a non-profit company that aims to develop and promote open standards across the visual effects community. The Association’s focus is to improve the OpenFX video image processing plug-in standard to reduce the level of development and support required for plug-ins across different compositing and editing host platforms.

Founded in 2004, The Open Effects Association was created to encourage open communication between hosts and plug-in developers, while establishing a uniform specification built to benefit artists and users. The API is owned by the association. By joining OFXA, members can network with fellow plug-in developers and collaborate to extend the OpenFX API.

For more information including how to join visit www.openeffects.org
